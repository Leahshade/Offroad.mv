About Us

Offroad is a clothing store that was founded in 2011 and to this date has been focused on providing customers with comfortable, affordable and quality clothes. We mainly focus on clothing for men but we also provide bags and footwear as well.

We mainly sell shirts from the well known brands Jack & Smith as well as REEF. We also sell Jeans from brands such as LEVI's, TBY, GUTZ and GO-UP. We also sell pants from brands such as Tommy Hilfiger, GO-UP and Zudio.

We always seek to bring new and affordable pieces of clothing to our customers.
